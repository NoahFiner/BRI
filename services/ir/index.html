Interventional Services

Our services include the following minimally invasive procedures (more detailed information at radiologyinfo.org)


▣ Performing arterial and venous angiograms and when necessary treating stenoses with angioplasty and stents.


▣ Embolization of post traumatic bleeding


▣ Locating areas of internal bleeding involving bowel utilizing angiography and embolization of bleeding vessels


▣ Revascularization/reconstruction of occluded arteries

▣ Embolization of kidney/spine/liver tumors prior to surgical resection to prevent significant blood loss during surgery


▣ Deep vein thrombosis - acute and chronic thrombolysis and recanalization


▣ Aortic stentgrafts: Treatment of thoracic aortic/abdominal aortic aneurysm and dissection


▣ Hemodialysis access management


▣ TIPS (Transjugular intrahepatic portosystemic shunt placement)


▣ Emergent/urgent carotid stenting


▣ Central vascular line placement and peripherally inserted central catheter placement (PICC lines)


▣ Chemoembolization of liver tumors


▣ Radiation treatment (Y90) of liver tumors


▣ Uterine fibroid embolization


▣ Varicose Vein Treatment


▣ Varicocele vein - diagnosis and comprehensive treatment


▣ Foreign body removal


▣ IVC Filter placement and removal


▣ Treatment of DVT, acute and chronic


▣ Treatment of massive pulmonary embolism


▣ Percutaneous gastric feeding tube placement (Gastrostomy)


▣ Percutaneous nephrostomy tube placement


▣ Percutaneous biliary drainage


▣ Blood patch


▣ Epidural injections


▣ Nerve root blocks (Cervical, Thoracic, Lumbar, Sacral, ganglion, peripheral)


▣ Discography


▣ Facet injections


▣ Bone tumor ablation using Cryotherapy or RF ablation


▣ Vertebroplasty (Thoracic and Lumbar)


▣ Image guided bone biopsy


▣ Refractory pleural effusion or ascites - Denver drain placement


▣ Osteoporotic insufficiency fractures - kyphoplasty, vertebroplasty, sacroplasty


▣ Pelvic pain in women - pelvic congestion syndrome treatment


▣ Hypertension - renal vein sampling


▣ Endocrine disorders - adrenal vein and cavernous sinus sampling


▣ Abscess drainage and catheter maintenance


▣ Placement of Denver drain for recurrent pleural and peritoneal fluid
