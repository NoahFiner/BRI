Our beginning

Boulder Radiologists, Inc. first began to work with Boulder Community Health, formerly known as Boulder Community Hospital, in 19__ . Through the years, our group has grown with Boulder Community Health from a core group of __ to thirteen radiologists. As the practice of radiology has evolved, we have diversified to include a number of specialities. We work closely with other physicians, nurses and technicians at Boulder Community Health to assure the best possible service to every patient.
