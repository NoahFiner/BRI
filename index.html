Who We Are

We are a group of thirteen radiologists that have been servicing the Boulder Community Health for many years. All of us are board certified and fellowship trained.

Learn more ➾

What We Do

To provide the highest level of care, each of the radiologists in our group is trained to handle both general issues as well as specialized areas in which we have additional training. We have three radiologists who specialize in minimally invasive procedures, two who focus on muscular skelatal imaging, two with further training in neuroradiology, as well as four of us who have fellowships in body imaging. We are all trained and experienced in mammography. To access the full range of our services, please scroll over the "services" heading on our website.
